Lakes and Green Spaces

Ambazari Lake & Garden

The largest lake in Nagpur, spanning 15 square kilometers, Ambazari is a haven for nature lovers. The adjoining Ambazari Garden offers a perfect setting for picnics and leisurely strolls. You can also enjoy boating facilities on the tranquil waters, especially during sunset.

Futala Lake

A 200-year-old man-made lake built by the Bhosle dynasty, Futala Lake is famed for its colourful fountains and illuminated surroundings in the evening. The landscape is surrounded by lush forests, making it one of the most picturesque spots for a carriage ride or a relaxing sunset view.

Seminary Hills

Seminary Hills in Nagpur is a green, hilly retreat considered the "lungs of the city," offering panoramic views, nature trails, and a blend of religious and recreational sites. It is named after the historic St. Charles Seminary and is popular with walkers, nature lovers, and those seeking a peaceful escape from the urban hustle.
